Global Opportunities

Shams Alwafa offers specialized services to help individuals and families pursue opportunities in Canada, Australia, and various European countries. Our experienced team provides guidance throughout the immigration process, ensuring a smooth transition to your destination of choice.

Canada

Canada offers numerous immigration pathways for skilled workers, entrepreneurs, and students. Our services are designed to help you navigate these options and find the program that best suits your qualifications and goals.

Our Canada Services Include:

  • Express Entry program assistance
  • Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP) guidance
  • Study permits and post-graduation work permits
  • Family sponsorship applications
  • Business immigration and entrepreneur programs

Our team stays updated on the latest immigration policies and requirements to provide you with accurate and timely advice.

Australia

Australia offers diverse opportunities for skilled professionals, investors, and students. We provide comprehensive assistance to help you understand and pursue the right visa pathway for your circumstances.

Europe

Europe offers diverse opportunities across multiple countries, each with its own immigration requirements and processes. Our services cover various European destinations and visa types to meet your specific needs.
